Questions about your account I want to change my email address To change your email address follow the steps below:
  1. Login to the website.
  2. Go to “My profile.”
  3. Click on the gear icon.
  4. You have arrived at the page where you can change your email address.
  5. Fill out your new email address twice and press “Save changes.”
  6. You receive an email with your new address. Click on the link to confirm your new address.
 I want to delete my profile To delete your profile follow the steps below:
  1. Login to the website.
  2. Go to “My profile.”
  3. Click on the gear icon.
  4. At the very bottom of the page click on the button “Permanent removal of account.”
  5. Click on “OK” to permanently remove your account.
 I do not want to receive email notifications To change your email notifications follow the steps below:
  1. Login to the website.
  2. Go to “My profile.”
  3. Click on the gear icon.
  4. Select which email notification you wish to receive.
  5. Click “save” to confirm your choice.
